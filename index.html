    About

    Longmont, Colorado's woodcrafter

    From small measuring spoons to stunning live edge coffee tables, every piece I craft is born from my passion for woodworking. What began as a hobby in my garage has grown into a bustling woodshop, where each project is made with care and attention to detail. No two pieces are the same, as each creation celebrates the unique beauty of the wood itself. I’m thrilled to now share my work with a broader audience. With your support, I can continue to grow, investing in more diverse tools and materials, allowing me to take on new, exciting projects and explore fresh possibilities in woodworking.

     

    Thank you for being a part of this journey!
